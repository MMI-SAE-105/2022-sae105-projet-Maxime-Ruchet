Canada

Why Canada ?

I chose this place because it is a country which attracts me enormously on the one hand by its progress technologies and on the other hand by its extraordinary landscapes. Moreover, this choice of place in my project is partly due to the fact that I dream of going to Canada one day in my future life. Dawn boreal, which is the best known phenomenon in Canada, is something that attracts me the most because of the beauty of the colors and the landscape it offers. Finally, Canada is a country that remains cold all year round.

A country with a promising future

I also find that it is a country with a great future, with great faith in the future of technology and ecology. It is a very wild country with magnificent landscapes. As I speak to you, I dream of working in Canada in a web development company and why not be a freelance web designer for various requests.

Magnificent landscapes!

I hope to live one day in Canada to be able to hike in the forests, especially in the Canadian boreal forest which seems to be a particularly beautiful place with its landscapes and its gigantism. The northern lights, which are the star phenomena of this country, are events that I dream of seeing one day in my life.

Canada's Challenges

I hope to one day live in Canada to hike in the forests, especially in the Canadian boreal forest which seems to be a particularly beautiful by its landscapes and its gigantism. Nowadays, we only talk about global warming and Canada is particularly affected since the continent is located in the northern hemisphere of planet Earth. I hope that in the future you can to see a change in ecological conditions and that humanity will have found a solution to stop this phenomenon.

Canada's flaws

The only complaint I can make about Canada is that football is not very developed in this country, unlike France. In Canada, it is hockey that is particularly practiced, while my favorite sport is football.
