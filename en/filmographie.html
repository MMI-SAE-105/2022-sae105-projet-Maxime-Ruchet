Gladiator

Gladiator was created in 2000 by Ridley Scott. It is a film based on the story of the Romans which takes place at the end of the 2nd century AD. We can see through this film a historical reality set up by the director.

Alien, the eighth passenger

Alien, the eighth passenger was created in 1976. It is a futuristic film based on the theme of space with the presence of extraterrestrials.

Thelma and Louise

Thelma and Louise is a comedy-drama film. The film tells us the story of two friends and their different tragedies.

Blade Runner

Blade Runner is a science fiction film created in 1982. The film features humans at war against droids. The action takes place in Los Angeles in 2019.

American Gangster

American Gangster is a film created in 2007. It is a detective film about the war between the different gangs located in the United States
