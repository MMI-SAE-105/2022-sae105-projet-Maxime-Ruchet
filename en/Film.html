alone on mars

Synopsis

Alone on Mars is a movie created in 2015 and directed by Ridley Scott. It's a futuristic movie of science fiction or simply science which tells the story of Mark Watney a astronaut stranded on mars. He is alone and must use his intelligence and logic survive alone on the planet mars.

More detailed filmography of the director

Why did you choose this film?

I chose this film because I think it sums up our current life very well, in particular Elon Musk's project to inhabit the planet Mars. This project is ongoing and testing is being conducted by the space x team led by Elon Musk. This film is very well done, very realistic and I even think that it played on the project of living on the planet. I asked myself a lot of questions, especially about my future life, that is to say the questioning about the habitation of the planet Mars. Also, to know whether or not this project can be realized one day...

A film full of technology

The film also touches on a part that attracts me enormously, which is technology. On the other hand, the ultra sophisticated astronaut suit as well as the high-tech spacecraft . In addition, we find in the film solar panels as well as a completely revolutionary system allowing to do gardening and thus to survive on Mars.

A role in advancing our future

The action of the film takes place in the year 2035. I can't wait to see where we will be in the advancement space colonization during this period. This is a subject that interests me. particularly interested and very curious to see how far we can go.

A top actor

The main actor of the film is Matt Damon. He is an American actor who starred in many movies like: "Saving Private Ryan", "Will Hunting" and many more.

The negative point

The negative point that I could retain from this film is the following. The director did not seek to return in more detail on the technical aspects of space jobs.

The positive points

I really liked the film for its staging, its special effects. It allowed me to learn a lot more about the colonization of Mars and the psychology of man to know how to survive alone in a totally unknown place.
