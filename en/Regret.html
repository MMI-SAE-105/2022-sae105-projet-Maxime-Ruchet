My regrets

Global warming on the rise!!!

Today we are in 2022 and global warming is the most important thing right now. Everyone is talking about it and panicking. Every year the the temperature within the planet is increasing more and more and the journalists are even talking of end of the world in a few years.

A new air of colonization

Elon Musk, whom I have already quoted in a previous paragraph, seeks to bring the man on the planet Mars so that we can live on this planet.

My biggest fear!

Later, I think global warming will get worse because of the speed at which it is changing. We talk a lot about global warming on social networks, news, news etc…. At the moment, temperatures exceed 40° in certain regions of France, which is very worrying. This is why I hope never to experience a sharp rise in heat or a heat wave over 50° because I hate being too hot.

A domino effect

In addition, global warming can have an impact on our environment because it is the consequence of the melting of the ice and therefore it causes a huge rise in water levels. global warming also causes premature deaths in the elderly. Due to the heat wave, it may affect the destruction of our ecosystem. That is to say serious consequences for the environment (fires, high temperatures and melting ice).

Solutions to adopt

I hope that one day we will find a way to reduce or even stop the global warming that affects the whole world. For this, many things are put in place. There are agreements with many countries on co2 consumption. Many countries are warning their people to avoid over-consumption. Many innovations are present to deal with this warming. For example, wind turbines, solar panels etc. However, for me these efforts are not accentuated enough and I would like a future world with even more efforts to counter this rise in global warming.
