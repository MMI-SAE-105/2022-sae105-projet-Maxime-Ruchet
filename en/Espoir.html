my hopes

The advancement of technology

Right now, my greatest hope for my generation is the development of Technology. Today, we are at the transition from the thermal car to the car electric. Our governments want a world with 100% electric cars in traffic including public transport etc... However, today I am quite skeptical about the subject because we have not yet found a way to recycle batteries used electric cars. I think many years from now we will see a world with electric cars whose particularity will be to have completely autonomous driving.

The transport of the future

Nowadays, transport prototypes are being developed. This kind of transportation would be like an autonomous car that moves through the air. This project is in finalization and I wonder whether or not this concept will be a revolution...

Phones in constant evolution

Every year, new prototype phones appear all over the world. I am passionate about new phones and I can't wait to know later about the developments made on these products. I hope to later have a more powerful system than fiber to make downloads and browse the web faster.

The electric brought to light

Nowadays, Elon Musk seeks to develop electronic chips inserted into our cranial box in order to access the metaverse in real time. I hope later not to witness this technology because at present I fear that it is not good for our mental health.

L'évolution de la technologie peut être une bonne chose d'un point de vue médical car elle permettra de guérir de nombreuses maladies mortelles.

However, many people can fall into phases of addiction and depression because of the internet. For me, these harmful effects must be monitored because technology is advancing at high speed.
